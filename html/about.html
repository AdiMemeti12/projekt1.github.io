About Kosovo Souvenirs


Kosovo Souvenirs is a trading name of Prishtina of Kosovo, founded originally in 1975 as a retailer of souvenirs and gifts in Kosovo.

Our retail shop at 3, Prishtina SW1A 2DD first opened in 1979, and we opened our online shop in 2001.

Kosovo Souvenirs first set up its wholesale business in late 1982 which has expanded rapidly so that we supply throughout the Kosovo and to many countries overseas
